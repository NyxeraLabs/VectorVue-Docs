VectorVue Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy defines the permitted and prohibited uses of VectorVue.

VectorVue is a controlled cybersecurity intelligence and operational platform intended exclusively for authorized security testing, analysis, and research.

2. Authorized Context

VectorVue may only be used:

  • Under explicit written authorization
  • With signed Rules of Engagement (RoE)
  • Within defined contractual scope
  • In compliance with applicable laws

3. Prohibited Activities

Users may NOT:

  • Use VectorVue for unauthorized access or exploitation
  • Deploy VectorVue against systems without written authorization
  • Conduct surveillance outside agreed scope
  • Disable logging, audit trails, or integrity controls
  • Attempt to bypass RBAC or approval workflows
  • Use the platform to create competing commercial services (per BSL 1.1 restrictions)

4. Infrastructure Misuse

It is strictly prohibited to:

  • Use VectorVue to attack public infrastructure
  • Target government systems without legal mandate
  • Conduct destructive or disruptive operations
  • Use data for extortion, harassment, or unlawful activities

5. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Immediate termination of access
  • Revocation of license
  • Legal action
  • Reporting to appropriate authorities

6. Acknowledgment

By installing, accessing, or operating VectorVue, you agree to comply with this Acceptable Use Policy.
